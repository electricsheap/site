Music Education
in the Time of COVID 19
Term Definition
Lived experiences: The experience of someone, and the knowledge they gained from said experience
Music education Any class, elective, or activity which centers around teaching students how to perform, create, or consume music.
Music educators Teachers or tutors who partake in music education with the purpose of teaching others
Music students Students who partake in music education with the purpose of learning
Teaching effectively Teaching students so that they engage with the material and retain the knowledge and benefits of the class.
Virtual education/classroom Teaching via internet-facilitated meetups, such as zoom meetings, and/or by asynchronous lessons and assignments
